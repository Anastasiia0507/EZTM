Are you looking for the solution to medical coordination and protection of your health data ?

Our benefits

To put in all hands

Intended for all players in sport, our universal health capsule is ideally integrated with the sports ecosystem and its legal environment. It optimizes the work of staffs on a daily basis. EZTM is both a field-based collaborative tool supporting medical performance expertise, but also a monitoring, longitudinal monitoring and decision support system.

Nothing is lost,everything is transformed !

EZTM fully meets the needs of professionals and does every day in the most specialized environments. It values and protects the work done while respecting the specificities and competences of each stakeholder and each institution.

By and for the field !

EZTM optimise la communication entre les différentes unités des staffs. Cette application collecte et sécurise les données sensibles et fournit des modules personnalisables en fonctions des besoins.

From the individual to the collective !

A software that can be adapted to all sports structures and practices: - Ligues / Fédés / Firmes: for study, research and statistical purposes. - Clubs / Pools / Teams / Squads: for management, steering and organisation purposes.

Sublime and subliminal !

The use makes it possible to model and periodically a tailor-made program with precision. Allowing better monitoring of injury, prevention and recovery processes. Exploitation allows the control of objectives and coordination of work units using relevant data collected in real time.

A saving of time !

The main purpose of creating the EZTM application was to create a tool that would save the user time. We have therefore created a modern and easy-to-use application for monitoring the health of athletes.
